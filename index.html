About Us

Who are we?

We are hackathon hackers dedicated to connecting students and hardware. As fellow hackers, we want you to have equipment so you can show off your talent and imagination!

What do we do?

We rent hardware to students who want to try out different items. Maybe you are unable to access something, or you simply want to try out a new product. Either way, we're here to help you get the equipment you need!

Why do we exist?

Many students need easier access to hardware, and we want to make that a reality. Information and advice is widely available, and your skills just need equipment to work on.

Are hackers dangerous?!

We strongly oppose the use of technology for criminal activities. At HackWare, we define "hackers" as students who innovate with software and hardware to create better technology. To see what students are making, please visit ChallengePost.

How does rental work?

We offer various rental prices based on delivery speed and shipping insurance. This lets you choose how fast the hardware gets to you! We will also open a credit card authorization for the full retail value of all items you rent. If you return all items on time and in the same condition as when we sent them to you, we will not charge you anything. This would be like getting a hotel room and taking care of it.

What if you break or keep the hardware?

We are here for all students. If you break it, you buy it. If you keep it, you buy it. We will charge you for the full retail value to maintain our inventory. If you return any hardware late, we will charge you. For the first two weeks, being late costs you 10% of the full retail value each week. Afterwards, it will cost you an additional 10% every day. At 100%, you get a strike. Three strikes, and you're out. See our terms and conditions to learn more.

How long can you rent hardware?

Sharing is caring, so the default rental period is 2 weeks. If there is not a waitlist for the item, you may extend your rental by 1 week, twice. At the end of your rental period, late charges will apply. In short, libraries have inspired us.

What if an item breaks during shipping?

We will always ship with insurance and a tracking number, and we advise you to do the same when returning the hardware. If anything happens to the hardware, either one of us will be able to file a claim with the post carrier for reimbursement.
